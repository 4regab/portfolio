## Overview

DeepTerm is an open-source, all-in-one study companion that gamifies the learning experience to boost efficiency. It combines AI-powered features with game mechanics to make studying more engaging and productive.

## Key Features

- **Gamified Learning**: Earn points, track streaks, and level up as you study — turning mundane study sessions into engaging experiences.
- **AI-Powered Assistance**: Leverages AI to generate study materials, flashcards, and quizzes tailored to your learning goals.
- **All-in-One Platform**: Consolidates notes, study tools, and progress tracking in a single interface.
- **Open Source**: Fully open-source, welcoming contributions from the community.

## Technologies Used

- **TypeScript**: For type-safe application logic.
- **Next.js**: For the web-based frontend with server-side rendering.
- **AI/ML APIs**: For intelligent content generation and study assistance.
